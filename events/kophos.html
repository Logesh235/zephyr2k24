KOPHOS

  1. A clip will be played showcasing a technological scene from a movie. The contestants have to figure out the kind of technology depicted in that particular scene.

  2. Interested people can participate in a team of 2.

  3. Participants can be from same or different colleges or branches.

  4. Use of mobile phones during competition are strictly prohibited.

  5. Each team should bring their own laptop.

  6. The decision of judges is final.
