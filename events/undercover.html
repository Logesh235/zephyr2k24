UNDERCOVER

DESCRIPTION:

  1. It is a word based game. It is an individual player game which has 3 characters namely Civilian, Undercover and Mr.White.
  2. The Civilians and Undercover characters only have a secret word. The secret word is similar for Civilians and Undercover but they were not same word.
  3. Mr.White does not have any word. But they have to guess the civilian word.
  4. Your goal is to find out the other playerâ€™s identities (and yours) as fast as possible to eliminate your enemies.
  5. Remember to keep your secret word hidden from others.

OBJECTIVES:

  1. Civilians win if they eliminate all Undercovers and Mr.Whites.
  2. The Imposters (Undercovers and Mr.Whites) win if they survive until only 1 Civilian is left.
  3. Mr.White also wins if they guess the Civilians word correctly.
